Privacy Policy — Pounce

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Short version: Pounce runs entirely in your browser. It does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There is no server, no analytics, no tracking.

1. What Pounce Does

Pounce is an open-source Chrome extension that lets you search across your open tabs, bookmarks, browser history, and top sites from a single keyboard-triggered overlay (⌘K / Alt+K). All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Data We Access

Pounce reads the following data from your browser only to display search results. It is never sent anywhere, stored on any server, or shared with third parties.

Browser APIPurpose
tabsRead titles and URLs of open tabs so you can switch to them.
bookmarksSearch your bookmark titles and URLs.
historyInclude recent browser history in search results.
topSitesSuggest frequently visited sites.
storageSave your URL list and theme preference locally (via Chrome storage).
scripting + activeTabInject the search overlay into the current page when you trigger the shortcut.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information of any kind.
  • We do not track which sites you visit or which results you click.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting.
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
  • We do not transmit any data to any external server. Pounce makes no network calls.

4. Where Your Data Lives

Local storage

Your saved URL list and theme preference are stored locally in Chrome via the chrome.storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

Chrome Sync

If you have Chrome Sync enabled in your Google account, Chrome may synchronize your saved URL list across your signed-in devices. This sync is handled entirely by Google. Pounce has no access to and no role in that synchronization.

5. Third-Party Services

Pounce integrates with no third-party services. It depends on the Chrome Extension APIs provided by your browser and nothing else.

6. Data Retention

Pounce retains no data on our side (we have no servers). Data stored locally by Pounce remains on your device until you clear it from the extension's settings or uninstall the extension. Uninstalling Pounce removes all locally stored data created by it.

7. Your Rights

Because Pounce does not collect or store any data outside your own browser, there is nothing for us to provide, export, or delete on request. You are in full control of all Pounce data — you can view, modify, or clear it directly from the extension at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Pounce is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information. It does not collect personal information from anyone.

9. Open Source & Verifiability

Pounce is MIT-licensed and fully open source. Every claim in this policy can be independently verified by reading the source code at github.com/TuYv/pounce.

10. Changes to This Policy
